## Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering (Grado en Ingeniería de Software) 2010 plan @ Universidad de Málaga


### Foundations & theory

* **Discrete mathematics & mathematical reasoning** — sets, logic, proofs, combinatorics, relations and functions; necessary for modeling and algorithm correctness. (Matemática Discreta, Cálculo para la Computación).
* **Calculus & linear algebra basics for computing** — derivatives, integrals, matrices used in algorithms, graphics and ML foundations. (Cálculo para la Computación, Estructuras Algebraicas).
* **Probability & statistics for computing** — basic stats, probability, applied to performance analysis and basic machine learning. (Métodos Estadísticos).
* **Theory of computation / formal languages & automata** — formal grammars, finite automata, regular expressions, pumping lemma, language decidability — foundations for compilers, parsers and formal verification. (Teoría de Autómatas y Lenguajes Formales).

### Programming & software construction

* **Imperative & object-oriented programming** — writing structured programs, classes, inheritance, polymorphism; idiomatic design and code organization. (Introducción a la Programación; Programación Avanzada I).
* **Data structures & collections** — arrays, lists, stacks, queues, trees, heaps, hash tables, graphs and their API usage and complexity tradeoffs. (Estructura de Datos).
* **Algorithms & complexity analysis** — algorithm design (divide & conquer, greedy, dynamic programming), asymptotic complexity, algorithm correctness and empirical performance. (Análisis y Diseño de Algoritmos; Algoritmia y Complejidad).
* **Concurrent & systems programming** — threads, processes, synchronization primitives, race conditions, concurrent data structures and models of concurrency. (Programación de Sistemas y Concurrencia).

### Software engineering core

* **Software engineering fundamentals** — software lifecycle models, engineering principles, requirements→design→implementation→testing→maintenance. (Introducción a la Ingeniería del Software).
* **Requirements engineering** — elicitation techniques, stakeholder analysis, writing SRS, specification and acceptance criteria. (Ingeniería de Requisitos).
* **Software modeling & UML** — UML diagrams (use case, class, sequence, component, deployment), architectural modeling and model-driven approaches. (Modelado y Diseño del Software; Ingeniería Web (UML/architectural modelling)).
* **Design patterns & architectural patterns** — common OO design patterns (Factory, Strategy, Observer, MVC, etc.) and architectural styles (layered, client-server, REST, microservices, SOA). (Modelado y Diseño del Software; Ingeniería Web; Tecnologías del Servidor).
* **Formal methods & formal specification** — formal reasoning about software, specification languages and mathematical techniques for verification. (Métodos Formales para la Ingeniería del Software).
* **Software testing & maintenance** — unit/integration/system testing, test design techniques, automated testing frameworks, regression testing, debugging and software maintenance strategies. (Mantenimiento y Pruebas del Software).
* **Software quality, metrics & assurance** — QA processes, quality models, reviews, static analysis, metrics and process improvement. (Calidad de Software; Gestión de Proyectos Software).

## Final Degree Project (TFG) 2025

Class Folder is a full-stack web application created as a bachelor’s thesis to offer students a more structured way to collaborate than WhatsApp or Telegram. It allows users to share files (with filters and search), track events via a calendar, engage in threaded discussions, and send private messages. It also includes a participation ranking system, private groups with permission settings, and moderation tools. The tech stack consists of Next.js and React on the front-end, Node.js/Express for the API, MySQL as the database, and JWT for authentication. The developer followed a waterfall methodology, building everything from scratch, and learned important lessons in usability, maintainability, and design by tackling real-world needs.
